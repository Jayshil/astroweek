About

AstroWeek

AstroWeek is a week long workshop on various topics of astrophysics originally organized by the Astronomy club of Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat-7, Gujarat, India. Although the founding members of this club are no longer affiliated to the institute, they keep the tradition of organizing this workshop.


In times of corona, we are organizing this workshop online on Zoom. This time we shall focus on various topics ranging from basics of astronomy to cosmology. The 2021 edition of AstroWeek is not only consist of undergraduate level lectures, but it will also contains the hands-on sessions to work on the real data gathered by various telescopes.
